1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners about how visitors interact with their sites.

Types of Tracking Technologies We Use:

  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device
  • Local Storage: Data stored in your browser for longer-term retention
  • Session Storage: Temporary data stored during your browsing session
  • Tracking Pixels: Tiny, invisible images used to track activity

7. MANAGING YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

Cookie Consent Banner

Our Site displays a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize cookie categories
  • Withdraw consent at any time

The banner will reappear if you clear your cookies or after 12 months to renew your preferences.

Browser Controls:

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Important Notes:

  • Blocking essential cookies may prevent the Site from functioning properly
  • Blocking affiliate cookies means we won't receive credit for purchases made through our links
  • Your preferences are stored in a cookie, so clearing cookies will reset them
  • Different browsers have different control options and locations

"Do Not Track" Signals:

Our Site does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals because no industry standard for implementation has been established. However, you can control tracking through the methods described above.
