1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide reporting information, and help personalize your experience.

2. WHY DO WE USE COOKIES?

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Operation: To enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance and Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our Site, count visits, and identify traffic sources. This helps us improve the performance and content of our Site.
  • Functionality: To remember your preferences (like language or region) for a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising/Affiliate Marketing: To track referrals from our Site to third-party product or service providers (our affiliate partners) so we may receive a commission if you make a purchase.

3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

Type of Cookie Purpose Who Serves it
Essential Cookies Necessary for the website to function. Cannot be switched off. Vercel (our hosting provider)
Analytics Cookies Allow us to count visits and traffic sources to measure and improve site performance. Google Analytics
Affiliate/Referral Cookies Track referrals to our affiliate partners (e.g., Digistore24). These are placed when you click on a product link. Third-party affiliate networks (e.g., Digistore24)

4. HOW LONG DO COOKIES LAST?

  • Session Cookies: Expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (e.g., 30 days to 2 years) or until you delete them.

5. HOW CAN YOU CONTROL COOKIES?

You have several options to control or limit how we and our partners use cookies:

Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences through their settings. You can usually find these controls in the "Options," "Tools," or "Preferences" menu. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that blocking Essential Cookies may affect the functionality of our Site.

Analytics Opt-Out

To prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

"Do Not Track" Signals

Our Site does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers because no uniform standard for recognition has been adopted.
