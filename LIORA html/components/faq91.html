FAQs

Transparency is the foundation of trust. Below, you’ll find clear answers to the most common questions about how LIORA Health works, how we’re funded, and how we can help you make informed decisions about your wellness.

What is LIORA Health, and how do you choose products?

LIORA Health is an independent editorial guide focused on the science of vitality after 40. We are not a product manufacturer or retailer. We research and review health supplements by analyzing the available clinical evidence for their ingredients, examining third-party quality verifications, and comparing value. Our goal is to cut through marketing claims and provide clear, science-backed information to help you make confident choices.

How is LIORA Health funded? Are your reviews biased?

LIORA Health is supported through affiliate partnerships. This means if you click a product link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Crucially, this does not influence our editorial process. Our methodology is to identify what we believe are the best-researched options first. We then check for affiliate availability, which might not always be possible. We do not recommend products based on commission rates, and we exclude products that don't meet our criteria, regardless of payout. Our credibility is our most important asset.

Is your "Science of Vitality After 40" guide really free? What do I get?

Yes, it is completely free. When you provide your email address, you will immediately receive a PDF download of our comprehensive 7-Day Reset Protocol. This evidence-based guide includes daily, actionable steps targeting circulation, cognition, metabolism, and recovery—the foundational pillars of sustained vitality. You will also join our community to receive science-backed wellness insights.

What does "Under Review" mean on your site?

Our "Products Under Review" section gives you a look at our active research process. It features supplements we are currently investigating for potential future recommendation. You can show interest in a product to help us prioritize our deep-dive reviews. It reflects our commitment to being a dynamic, responsive resource rather than a static list.
